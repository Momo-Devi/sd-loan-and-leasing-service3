Fully Maintained Operating Lease: Comprehensive Leasing for Businesses

What is a Fully Maintained Operating Lease?

A Fully Maintained Operating Lease is a leasing option where all maintenance, servicing, and repairs are included in the lease agreement. This provides businesses with predictable costs and ensures that leased assets remain in top condition without additional expenses.

Key Benefits of a Fully Maintained Operating Lease

  • Predictable Costs: Fixed monthly payments cover leasing and maintenance expenses.
  • Hassle-Free Maintenance: Includes servicing, repairs, and even tire replacements.
  • Improved Cash Flow: No unexpected costs for breakdowns or repairs.
  • Access to Newer Models: Upgrade to the latest vehicles and equipment at the end of the lease.
  • End-of-Lease Flexibility: Options to extend, return, or renew the lease with new assets.

How Does a Fully Maintained Operating Lease Work?

The process is simple and efficient:

  1. Choose the vehicle or equipment best suited for your business.
  2. Select a lease term with fixed monthly payments.
  3. All maintenance, servicing, and repairs are included during the lease period.
  4. At the end of the lease, return or upgrade to a newer model.

Who Should Consider This Lease?

A Fully Maintained Operating Lease is ideal for businesses that want to manage costs effectively while ensuring their fleet and equipment are always in top working condition.
